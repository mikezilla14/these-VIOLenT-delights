# World Tech & Gadgets

## Arc 01: The Proto-Tech (Gritty/Industrial)
*   **The Cyber-Mastiff:** A Rottweiler with crude hydraulic shears for jaws and exposed wiring. Vulnerable to magnets/frequency jamming.
*   **The Haptic Dress:** Electric blue smart-fabric that measures biometrics to prove "specimen quality" to remote buyers.
*   **The Copper Beeches:** Genetically modified trees with metallic leaves acting as a Faraday cage.

## Arc 02: The Invisible Suite (Sleek/Glass)
*   **The Halo:** Violet's AR Monocle. Overlays thermal residue and biometric stress levels.
*   **The Mimic Compact:** A makeup case that 3D prints stolen fingerprints into translucent film.
*   **The Siren Choker:** A silver necklace containing an inductive data spike and localized signal jammer.

## The Hollows (Arc 2 Location)
*   **Concept:** A submerged "aquarium" for cyborgs stripped of emotions (Limbic Dampening).
*   **The Governor Module:** A chrome port at the base of the skull that flattens cortisol/dopamine.
