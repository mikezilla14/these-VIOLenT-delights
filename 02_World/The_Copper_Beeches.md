**Jephro's Solution**: "The Human Firewall."

*   **The Clues (For the Reader)**
**The Keys**: Eudora carries physical iron keys. Why? Because the attic locks aren't digital. This hints that whatever is in there (Alice) needs to be kept off the grid.
**The Whistle**: Silas uses a high-frequency analog whistle for the dog. Violet realizes later she can mimic this frequency using a hacked phone speaker.
**The Food**: Violet sees Eudora taking a tray of food upstairs. It contains "soft food" (gruel/mash).
**Violet's Deduction**: "Jephro eats steak. Clara eats salads. Beatrix doesn't eat. Who is the soft food for?" (Answer: Alice, who has a jaw infection/neural lace rejection).
